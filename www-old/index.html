About The Building

Wellington House comprises a detached office building of steel frame constructions. It is arranged over ground and 7 upper floors totaling 43,000 sq.ft. The accommodation has been refurbished during the last two years.

The property benefits from the following specification:

  • Open plan, flexible floor plates.
  • Two passenger lifts serving all floors.

The site extends to approximately 1.3 acres and provides surface car parking with 100 spaces.

The building comprises of both Serviced and Non-Serviced offices. Rates for the serviced offices are inclusive of electricity, gas, water and service charge.
