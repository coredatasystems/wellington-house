Colchester Offices

Serviced Offices

Our serviced offices offer business rates for premium suites in the Wellington House building. The rates for serviced offices all include electricity, gas, water, and service charge. Offices are furnished, and further furniture can be supplied if required. Other benefits of our serviced offices include:

  • Discount on Meeting Room Access
  • Mail sorting
  • Manned reception (between 8:30am and 5pm)
  • CCTV cameras in operation
  • Call forwarding
  • Included car park space

Serviced suites are available with sizes ranging from 150 to 965 square feet.

Non-Serviced Offices

Our non-serviced offices are large, unfurnished open plan offices that can be adapted easily to your needs. Rates for serviced offices do not include utilities, which are offered as a separate service charge. Benefits of our non-serviced offices include:

  • Discount on Meeting Room Access
  • Mail sorting
  • Manned reception (between 8:30am and 5pm)
  • CCTV cameras in operation
  • Call forwarding
  • Included car park space

Non-serviced offices are available with sizes ranging from 150 to 965 square feet.

Virtual Offices

Our virtual office services allow you to hold a business address at Wellington House, without renting physical space. We provide the following for users of this service:

  • Discount on Meeting Room Access
  • Business Address
  • Mail Sorting
  • Mail Forwarding
  • Meeting Room Access

For more information and pricing, please visit our virtual offices page.
