Virtual Offices

Our virtual office services allow you to hold a business address at Wellington House, without renting physical space. The service offers numerous benefits, including mail sorting and forwarding, telephone answering and access to meeting rooms when necessary.

Business Address

If you work from home, you may want a non-residential address to register your business at for a number of reasons. Our virtual office service allows you to register your business at Wellington House, as well as receive mail to the address and book meeting rooms to meet clients.

Mail Forwarding

We sort and hold all of the mail sent to your registered address, which you can collect whenever is convenient for you. We can also forward this mail for you on a regular basis to any address. You can be notified whenever you receive any mail.

Meeting Room Access

We also provide the option to hire meeting rooms for a day or a number of hours whenever needed. Meeting rooms are equipped with plug sockets and projectors for giving important presentations.

Pricing

The virtual office service is provided at a cost of £50pcm or £500pa
